Client Consent for Use of Soma Platform

Your mental health professional uses a digital platform called Soma to support clinical documentation, workflows, and session tools. Please read this carefully before signing.

1. Use of Soma
Your provider uses Soma to assist with decisions, drafting notes and securely sharing session information. All clinical decisions and documentation remain the sole responsibility of your provider.

2. Anonymity

  • Soma does not collect or store your name, email, or personal identity when you sign up or use the app.
  • Your provider uses an alias (e.g., “Client A”) that cannot be used to identify you. They may store an identifier for you in their own secure records or secure systems.
  • It is possible that servers you encounter while using Soma collect your IP address in server logs. We do not access these logs or use IP addresses to identify you.

3. Audio Recording
By signing this form, you give explicit consent for your sessions to be audio recorded only for transcription purposes. These recordings are used solely to create a transcript that may be summarized for clinical note drafts. Recordings are not stored beyond 25 days, and any recordings or transcripts will not be used for any other purpose without your further consent.

4. Use of the Soma Mobile App and Anonymous Data
If you use the Soma mobile app, you consent to the collection of anonymous information you choose to enter, including:

  • Mood and subjective stress ratings
  • Time, date, and duration of entries
  • Completion state of activities

We will not access, use, or decrypt your encrypted journal entries or encrypted activity details without your explicit consent.

5. Wearable Device Data
If your provider requests wearable device data, the following applies:

  • This data remains anonymous.
  • Your wearable account credentials are encrypted, and we will not decrypt them to identify you even if you have consented to us using your data to improve the product.
  • The types of data that may be collected include heart rate, oxygen levels, breathing rate, sleep data, temperature, and activity data.
  • We will summarize and share this data with your provider only if you have explicitly consented to connect a wearable device.
  • Your provider may enable wearable connection as an option, but you can still choose to refuse.
  • If you consent to anonymous wearable data collection, Soma may use this data to improve the product in addition to any other data you have consented to share for improvement purposes.

6. Data Use

  • We follow Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA).
  • Your data is not sold or shared with third parties.
  • Only anonymous completion states, time, date, duration, and subjective ratings are collected and decrypted unless you opt in to share more.
  • Audio recordings, transcripts, activity details, and journal content are encrypted and not used unless you opt in.
  • If you opt in through the app settings, Soma may use your anonymous data to improve the system and its functionality.

7. Data Storage
All your data is anonymous and stored securely on servers located in Canada. Sensitive data — including journal details, task text, and wearable account credentials — is encrypted. We do not use this data unless you give explicit consent.

8. Connecting to Your Provider Through Soma
If you connect your app account to your provider:

  • You decide what to share through opt-in features.
  • Once shared, your provider is responsible for storing and using the data according to applicable laws and professional standards.
  • Disconnecting stops future sharing but does not delete data already stored in your provider’s records.
