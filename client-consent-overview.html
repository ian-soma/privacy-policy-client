Soma Client App – Consent
 Before you start, please review and agree to the following:

☐ I understand Soma is not a medical device or medical advice.
 Soma is a self-tracking tool. Only a qualified health professional can provide clinical advice or treatment. Soma does not diagnose or treat conditions.

☐ I understand my data is anonymous to Soma.
 Soma does not collect my name, email, or other identifiers. If I connect to a provider, they may keep my real identity in their own secure records.

☐ I control what I share.
 All sharing features are off by default. I choose what to enable, and I can turn them off anytime. Turning off sharing stops future transfers but not data already shared with my provider.

☐ I understand how my journal and activity data are handled.
 Journal entries and activity details remain encrypted unless I choose to share them with my provider or for anonymized product improvement.

☐ I understand wearable features are optional.
 These are not available in the free version. If my provider enables them, I can choose whether to connect a wearable.

☐ I understand session recording is optional and only available if my provider enables it.
If enabled and used, recordings are made only for transcription to assist with clinical notes, deleted within 25 days, and never used for any other purpose without my further consent.

By tapping “I Agree”, I confirm I’ve read and understood these points and the full Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
