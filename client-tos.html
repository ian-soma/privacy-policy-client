Free App Users:

2. Anonymity

In this document, “anonymous” means Soma does not store or link your personal identifiers (such as name or email) to your app data.

  • Your provider may store your real identity in their own secure clinical records — we cannot access this.
  • Our servers may receive your IP address in routine server logs, but we do not use or access IP logs to identify you.

5. Data Collected Through the App

Journal responsibly — do not include personal identifying information in your journal entries.

We never sell or share your data with third parties. Your data always remains anonymous, even if you consent to decryption of activity details and journal entries to improve the product and features.

If your provider requests wearable device data:

  • Your wearable account credentials are encrypted — we do not decrypt them even if you consent to using your data for product improvement.
  • Wearable data will only be summarized and shared with your provider if you consent to connect a wearable.
