Soma Client Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

Introduction

Soma Health Solutions Inc. ("Soma," "we," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Soma Client app.

Information We Collect

  • Journal Entries: Text and voice-recorded content you create in the app.
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings used only for transcription purposes.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve functionality.

Privacy Protection Through De-identification

  • All your data is de-identified before storage or processing
  • We only know you by the pseudonym or alias assigned by your mental health professional
  • We take extensive measures to ensure your de-identified information cannot be re-identified
  • Your identity is known only to your mental health professional, not to Soma

Client Data Types

  • Information from your Mental Health Professional: May include pseudonymized identifiers, session notes, or transcriptions
  • Your Direct Entries: Journal entries, subjective ratings, and records of recurring tasks that you voluntarily provide

Voice Recording & Transcription

When you use voice input:

  • We temporarily process audio to convert it to text for your journal entries
  • Audio recordings are not permanently stored after transcription
  • Recordings are never shared with third parties
  • Transcribed text is securely synced with your de-identified account

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide journaling services between therapy sessions
  • To sync your journal entries with your mental health professional
  • To improve the app's functionality and user experience
  • To maintain the security of your account

Data Security

  • Your de-identified information is stored on secure servers
  • We use encryption to protect your data
  • Access is limited to authorized personnel only

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy: Email: support@soma-health.ca

By using the Soma Client app, you agree to this privacy policy.
